Capabilities

One app that handles it all

Stitch connects to the tools you already use and automates the work you do every day — right from your desktop.

File management

Organize, rename, move, and search through files and folders. Stitch works with your local documents, spreadsheets, PDFs, and more.

Email & calendar

Connect Gmail and Google Calendar. Stitch can read, draft, and send emails, manage your schedule, and handle routine communications.

Web browsing

Research topics, look up information, fill out forms, and pull data from websites — Stitch drives a real browser on your behalf.

Scheduled automations

Set tasks to run on a schedule. Daily email summaries, weekly file cleanups, monthly report generation — automate the repetitive stuff.

Plain language

No code, no complex setup. Just tell Stitch what you need in everyday language and it figures out how to get it done.

Works out of the box

Download, open, and start working. No lengthy onboarding, no configuration wizards. Connect an AI provider and you're ready.

Privacy & trust

Your data never leaves your machine

Most AI tools send your files, emails, and documents to the cloud. Stitch doesn't. Everything runs locally on your computer, and the entire codebase is open for anyone to inspect.

100% local processing

Stitch runs on your computer, not in the cloud. Your files, emails, and documents are processed locally and never uploaded to our servers. There are no servers.

No accounts or tracking

No sign-ups, no telemetry, no analytics. Download the app and start using it immediately. Stitch doesn't know who you are and doesn't want to.

Fully open source

Every line of code is public on GitHub. Audit what it does, build it from source, or contribute. No black boxes, no proprietary backends, no trust required.

You approve every action

Stitch asks for your permission before doing anything sensitive. You see exactly what it plans to do and can say no. You're always in the loop.

Bring your own AI provider

Connect your own API key from OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google — or run a local model for fully offline use. Your API key talks directly to the provider, not through us.

Permission controls

Define rules for what the AI can and can't do. Restrict access to specific folders, block destructive commands, or require approval for every action.
